Privacy Policy

DueBar — Last updated June 2026

DueBar is a macOS menu bar app that displays your upcoming Google Tasks. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data accessed

DueBar requests read and write access to your Google Tasks via the Google Tasks API (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/tasks). This allows the app to:

  • Display your tasks and due dates in the menu bar
  • Mark tasks as complete
  • Create new tasks
  • Reschedule task due dates

Data storage

DueBar does not transmit your task data to any server. All data is fetched directly from Google's servers to your Mac and displayed locally. OAuth tokens are stored in your Mac's Keychain and are never shared.

Data sharing

DueBar does not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party. The app communicates only with Google's APIs on your behalf.

Revoking access

You can sign out at any time from within the app, which removes your tokens from the Keychain. You can also revoke access entirely at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
